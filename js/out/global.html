(constant) flowers

The pictures/values for the cards Unicode characters were used so the client doesn't have to download the images. This improve loading time... less data has to be downloaded. This is especially helpful for players who are in areas with poor internet connectivity. The Unicode characters are displayed differently on different devices. This is a positive for uniqueness but a negative for consistency in design and experience.
