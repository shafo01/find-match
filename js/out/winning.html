(static) createWinningScreen()

Create and display the winning screen to the user. It fades out the player's playing board and display the winning screen: Tell the player he has won and display link to play again. The winning screen display one of the flowers in the games for decoration three times. The number of moves, stars and seconds the player has at the end of the game.
